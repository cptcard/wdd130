Overview

Purpose

This sites purpose is to build revenue and share information about potential rafting trips. Ex: To enjoy a hot summer one can find themselves in the need of some adventure and some refeshing water. Jump into the river with our trained rafting crew and enjoy a summer full of fun. With our planning site we can find not only the route you wanna take but keep you in contact with our guides that can get you along your thrilling journey. What are you waiting for hop on in with us.

Audience

Our target audience is mostly middle to young age persons. This can include families which have a variety of ages and people. Which comes with risk and increased information on the website for all ages. People come to a rafting site to feel adventurous without all the danger of solo water expiditions. This means we should focus on saftey and showing how the guides can handle a little bit of water. The questions they will ask will include where are we going? What risk is involved? Is my healthiness a concern? Is my age a problem? What is provided? Who is our guide? What equipment will we use? What classes of rapids are we handling? What is the plan with each route? Most of the needs are food, saftey, and peace of mind. They can get this at our site through maps, videos, images, and documentation. They should be able to access the site through phone, desktop, or laptop. Ex problem solving: We take those of all ages. However there are some restrictions when it comes to where we can go. All are welcome on our raft however, know that we will have to keep to certain routes based of the health conditions of the group. There is also certain health conditions that may not allow a person on our tour of the river such as heart conditions and pregnacy. To better understand our policy please read the health and saftey guide. Where are we going? There are a variety of routes we can take based off of what skill level your group is at. Take a look at the begginer, easy, and medium maps linked below. Each map includes the total length and time we will be on the water. They also include each set of rapids and there leveling. You can also call us if you have any extra questions. Are we eating? Depending on the legth of the trip that we are taking there might be an hour break that the group will take about half way through the route. The cost of food will be calculated into your total before the trip is taken. What is available to eat. The crew packs sandwhiches for the entire group with many chocies of meats and condiments. Including options for vegan and gluten free. Also they will pack some chips for individual consumtion, plus a dessert usually cake or brownies.
